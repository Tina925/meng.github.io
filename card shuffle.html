CARD SHUFFLING PROJECT

This is a project I worked on in my freshman year. In order to improve safety and increase mobility around campus, we designed a speed trap sensor to put in intersection on campus in order to allow pedestrians to be warned if bikes or any other oncoming traffic is comming. My group did a thorough background research and used protottping, testing, and weighted decision matric (WDM) to construct the final design.
